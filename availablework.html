Available Work

Art is currently available by commission and throughout the year at select events and Pop-Ups. Collaborations with local artists and businesses are a core part of Erin’s practice. A few of these special collaborations are available year round with the partners listed below. For interior design projects and restaurants inquiries please email Erin at: erin@erinhuppceramics.com

Commissions

Commissions for table lamps, mirrors, pendant lighting, pedestal bowls, cake stands, wall installations and other select pieces are available throughout the year. For more details and to get started please complete a Commissions Form.

Bracelet Vases

Erin’s Ink Bracelet Vases are available at the San Francisco store Metier, which offers finely curated antique & modern jewelry, objects & art. Please inquire directly with Metier for availability.

Mt. Tamalpais Ring Dish and Ring

Erin’s collaboration with Amanda Hunt features a limited edition hand-thrown ring dish and ring set that honors Marin’s iconic Mount Tamalpais. Erin’s ring dish, inspired by the soft curves and lunar glow of its namesake, perfectly complements Amanda’s beloved AH Aria Ring, offered in 14k gold with an ethically sourced diamond.
